Studios

LifeHUB Studios are focused destinations that are simultaneously classrooms, gyms, kitchens, stages, virtual coffee shops, brainstorming nooks, etc. where members can learn about, experience and share wellness pursuits that are important to the enrichment of their lives.

Designed to embrace wellness (Physical. Mental. Social. Educational. Creative.), fitness, healthcare and education as creative elements that belong to the same space, LifeHUB Studios curate and share wellness content of all kinds with LifeHUB members through the LifeHUB app.

Our Studios

Fit Studio

Complete with a technical platform that tracks physical fitness activity, encourages goal-setting, and allows for participation in challenges, LifeHUB's Fit Studio offers a wide range of live and on-demand fitness and movement classes.

Art Studio

From drawing and painting, to music, performance, and other creative outlets, LifeHUB`s Arts Studio events bring out the imaginative spark in everyone.

Culiary Studios

Whether in your own kitchen or in a hosted studio space, LifeHUB Culinary Studio options are food adventures not to be missed. The Culinary Studio at LifeHUB is a mouth-watering source of cooking and nutrition classes and collaborations.

Learning Studio

Personal enrichment? Mindfulness meditation? A certification needed to move ahead in your career? Need advice on how to approach hard conversations? LifeHUB's Education Studio is where you can work on your mind and gain perspective to orient your life.

Music Studio

Always wanted to learn the guitar, piano, or ukulele? Or has it bee a few years since you picked it back up? Whether your learning an instrument for the first time, or wanting to progress in your songwriting or theory, LifeHUB's Music Studio will help you prepare for that next gig or just reconnect with playing around the house.

Proffesional Studio

Whether you want to learn about team-building, portfolio curation, public speaking, or presentation technology skills, LifeHUB's Professional Studio can help you level up in your career and help you offer more to those you work with.

Though current access to LifeHUB Studio classes is through the LifeHUB app, LifeHUB Studios can be hosted in physical locations of all sorts.
