Combining the facets of wellness and health management into a single, accessible platform, LifeHUB was created to share enriching membership experiences around all aspects of wellness - physical, mental, social, educational and creative.

Starting with a robust physical fitness live class schedule and library of on-demand programming, working off the foundation of fitness programming developed by Technogym, LifeHUB is much more than a physical wellness portal.

LifeHUB is the place for people to gather to build on their wellness journey - going beyond physical fitness options to include personal and professional development programs; creative studios that explore art, music, cooking, etc.; and conversation groups where guided interactions and experiences can be shared to advance social and cultural wellness dialog.

The LifeHUB model starts virtual, with access to the LifeHUB app where members access a full library of live and on-demand LifeHUB Studios experiences. Custom classes and specialized community studios can be created to meet more specialized community needs - for employers, dispersed organizations, and campus environments desiring wellness-focused interactions for their people.

Through on demand and live experiences, personal coaching and interaction, LifeHUB is a wellness community platform to enrich members' lives.

With physical fitness and engagement the foundation of a healthy lifestyle, the LifeHUB development team includes a physical therapy and rehabilitation expert; a developer and visionary who has spent decades creating compelling community centers; education, lifestyle and engagement leaders; and skilled technology professionals from various industries. This dynamic team is actively building LifeHUB Studios into a compelling and convenient destination to access and manage wellness solutions.

“Many of our interested audiences are looking for an easy tool to access their personal wellness options, a platform that helps individuals take more control over personal healthcare options, a solution that goes far beyond physical fitness tracking into education, cultural and life pursuits that add to mental wellness and overall well-being. LifeHUB is that platform. We`ve created a boundary-less space via the LifeHUB app that is an inclusive community gathering point for wellness interaction - physical, social, mental, creative, educational - literally any wellness pursuit that the LifeHUB community wants to explore.”

- Robert Dykstra, founder of LifeHUB.
